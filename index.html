About 8 Ball & Arrows

For the past 8 years, we've grown from a small venue with just 6 pool tables into a full sports bar with 14 competition-level tables, 6 dart board setups, in house Perri Perri Diner serving some top quality food, and TNT Sports on multiple screens. Whether you're here to play, eat, watch, or just relax, you’re in the right place.

  • Walk-ins always welcome
  • Pre-bookings accepted for pool tables, dart boards, and food
  • Fresh food served daily from our own kitchen
  • Wide variety of alcoholic and non-alcoholic drinks
  • Small outdoor seating area available

Comfort Guaranteed

The entire venue is fully air conditioned, keeping the temperature comfortable all year round.

Whether it's summer or winter, you can focus on enjoying the game, watching live sports, or relaxing with friends—without worrying about the weather outside.
